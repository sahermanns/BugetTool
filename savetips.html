10 Simple Ways To Trim Your Budget!

  • 1) Wake up an hour earlier to give yourself extra time.
  • 2) Dont drive unless you have to.
  • 3) Make your morning coffee at home.
  • 4) Pack your lunch on workdays.
  • 5) Make a grocery list before shopping and dont shop hungry.
  • 6) Set up a fixed automatic transfer to savings before you get a chance to touch it.
  • 7) Take cash out for your entertainment/alcohol fund so you are more aware of your spending.
  • 8) Make a point to cook at home most week nights.
  • 9) Identify your spending weaknesses and evaluate them from an annual perspective.
  • 10) Make savings goals to incentivize better budgeting.
